Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2023

Thank you for using RepsNear ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Core Principle: RepsNear is a local-first application. We do not require you to create an account. We do not store your personal data, customer lists, or sales records on our servers. Your data stays on your device.

1. Information Collection and Usage

Because RepsNear is an offline-capable app, we do not collect or store your personal information on external servers.

  • User Data: Data you input (such as customer profiles, tasks, and sales products) is stored locally on your device's internal storage.
  • Images: Photos you take or upload are saved securely in the app's document sandbox on your device.

2. App Permissions

To provide specific features, RepsNear requests access to certain system permissions. You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

  • Camera: Used to scan business cards for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and to take photos of customer storefronts. Images are processed locally or transiently via secure APIs (see Section 3) and are not stored by us.
  • Photo Library: Used to allow you to upload existing photos for avatars or customer profiles, and to save generated reports (Excel/Images) to your device.
  • Location: Used only while the app is in use to display your current location on the map, calculate distances to customers, and assist with route planning. We do not track or store your location history on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. However, the app may use trusted third-party services to support specific functionalities:

  • Google Gemini API (Generative AI): If you use the "AI Business Card Scan" feature, the text data recognized from the image is sent to Google's generative AI service for formatting (e.g., extracting Name/Phone accurately).
